/campaigns/new New Campaign — Product Search Form Internal team
  • Multi-select product dropdown (searchable, shows HSN + CAS inline)
  • HSN Code + CAS Number (auto-filled from product selection, editable)
  • No. of transactions per year (number input)

/campaigns/:id/leads Campaign Lead List Internal team
  • Table with checkboxes — SELECT ALL + individual select
  • Columns: Company, Country, Stage badge, Primary contact (email/phone), Last action date
  • Stage filter tabs across top (All / Discovered / Intro Sent / Pricing / Meeting / Closed)
  • Bulk actions bar on selection: "Send Intro Email", "Export Missing Contacts CSV"
  • Click row → opens Lead Detail drawer/sidebar
  • Missing contact badge (red) if no email AND no phone
/leads/:id Lead Detail — Customer Dashboard Internal team
  • Progress bar across top showing current stage (7 steps)
  • Company info card (from Volza) + Pricing trend + Purchase history
  • Contact cards — all contacts with email/phone, primary badge
  • Email tab: full thread history (in + out), "Send Email" button, AI draft panel
  • Call tab: chronological call log, "Log Call" button with notes field
  • Timeline tab: all LeadActions in order — the journey flowchart
  • Pause automation toggle (auto_flow_paused boolean)
  • "Close Deal" button → modal with won/lost + remarks
/products Products — Manage & Upload Brochures Internal team
  • Product list: name, HSN, CAS, brochure status (uploaded / missing)
  • "Add Product" form: name, HSN code, CAS number, description, technical specs
  • Brochure upload: drag-and-drop PDF upload per product
  • Preview brochure PDF inline before confirming
  • Edit / delete product
/ai-drafts AI Draft Approval Queue Internal team
  • List of all pending AI email drafts awaiting review
  • Each card shows: lead company, original customer email, Gemini draft response
  • Inline edit the draft before approving
  • "Approve & Send" → sends via Gmail SMTP, logs action
  • "Reject" → discards draft, optionally add note why
  • Badge count on nav item showing pending count
